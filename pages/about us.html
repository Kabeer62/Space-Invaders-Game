Welcome To My Website

Space Invaders is a classic arcade video game created by Kabeer Kumar. It is one of the most iconic and influential video games in the history of gaming. In Space Invaders, players control a laser canon that moves horizontally across the bottom of the screen, shooting at waves of descending aliens. The objective is to destroy all the invaders before they reach the bottom of the screen. As the game progresses, the aliens become faster and more challenging to defeat.
